SAFERELL ------Keep your umbrella in a safe condition.

RESEARCH

In rainy days, most of us take umbrellas when go out. Umbrellas keep us from being wet. I observed that people usually put the umbrellas outside the classrooms or the shops. During this process, it may occur many problems.

Many people had the experience of losing their umbrellas, maybe outside the shop, maybe inside the cinema, or maybe in the basket of mobike.
